INFORMATION ABOUT BAKED BEANS COFFEE SHOP

Why our coffee?
The answer is simple, the taste of our coffee can't be beaten.
We have had over 100 taste testers come and tell us which coffees tasted better,
and since then, we have done nothing but improve it for you. Also, we're rated 5 stars with over 2500 reviews!



Our history:
The baked beans coffee shop has been open since august 23rd of 2021.
The reason for it being newly opened is because covid had delayed the opening,
however we are here now and are happy to serve you!



CEO:
The CEO of this amazing coffee shop is Maher El-Merhebi. Maher grew
up in northern Lebanon and at the age of 16, he helped his father sell coffee.
After moving to Canada and entering the studies of business, he graduated and
decided to open up a coffee shop to honour his father, and named it the
"Baked Beans Coffee Shop" as a joke that only he and his father know about.



We open early to offer our clients flexible hours in order to get your coffee right before work!
Our staff are all early birds who will greet you with a smile to
start off your day joyfully, and who knows, you might feel adventurous and taste our traditional
turkish coffee! If you feel like leaving a review about our shop
and service, please visit our contact page.
